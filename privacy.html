The smart contracts and the Site are collectively referred to in this Policy as the “App”. By using the App, you agree that we can collect, use, disclose, and process your information as described in this Policy. This Policy only applies to the App, and not to any other websites, products or services you may be able to access or link to via the App. We encourage you to read the privacy policies of any other websites you visit before providing your information to them.

Information Collection

First and foremost, this app is fully-distributes, which means msgblocks doesn't have a custom back-end or database. It is basically just a front-end to the Ethereum blockchain. Therefore, we don't directly collect any of your data and don't store it outsite of the blockchain. Remember that the blockchain is public, so the messages you write will be public as well and stored on thousands of computers around the worls. They would be easily retrievable with a blockchain transaction explorer, and although you can obfuscate them with a password, the encrypted data will still be easily retrievable.

In order to save a message to the blockchain, you will need to use your Ethereum wallet through the MetaMask browser plugin. The Site will know what your wallet address is, but will not record it outside of the blockchain. The Site also asks for your name, as an optional information that will be stored on the blockchain together with your message. However, that can be any name you want to have associated to your message, it can be a nickname or some other form of identification, and you can also not provide one at all. In case you do provide that information, we will not record it outside of the blockchain anyway (just like for the wallet address).

To reiterate, we do not collect any other personally-identifiable information about you, unless you give it to us directly: by giving us written feedback, communicating with us via third party social media sites, or otherwise communicating with us via the App or any other means. We do not collect any payment information from you, other than your Ethereum wallet address.

We do however collect basic analytics when you use the App. This sort of information may be collected from third-party providers like Facebook or Google Analytics, and includes, among other things, the type of device you use, access times, IP address, hardware model, operating system and version, and other unique device identifiers. Some of these third-party providers may place cookies or pixels - small data files stored on your hard drive or in device memory - on your browser or hard drive. Note that this Policy does not cover the use of cookies or pixels by such third parties. Most web browsers are set to accept cookies and pixels by default, but you can usually set your browser to remove or reject browser cookies or pixels. If you do choose to remove or reject cookies or pixels, however, your ability to use the App might be affected.
